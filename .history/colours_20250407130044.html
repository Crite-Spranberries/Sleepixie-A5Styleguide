COLOURS

With the premise of being a sleep app, sleepixie's colour palette was built on serene pastel-like colours intended to help users feel at ease. The primary colours of select blues, yellows, and greys resonate with calmness, peace, serenity, and hope. The day mode colours follow the same format, in addition to the subtle cream off-white for added contrast.
