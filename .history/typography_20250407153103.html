TYPOGRAPHY

The Sleepixie typeface is built on the "Teachers" sans-serif typeface from google fonts. This was chosen to capture and reflect the soft mood the app aims for. The typeface is well known for its usage with teachers, publishers, and students. Below are multiple figures to display the fonts in action.
