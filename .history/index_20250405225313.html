LOGO

The Sleepixie logo is visualized with connecting shapes outlining the letter ‘S’ from our app name ‘Sleepixie’ The shapes signify the connecting help Sleepixie offers toward building sleep habits. The calm gradient blue tones and color of the shapes along with the yellow contrasting connection were chosen to give the user a warm, welcoming atmosphere signifying serenity, trust, energy, and optimism.
